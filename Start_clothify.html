Upload Your Clothes

Photo Upload:
- Users can take or upload photos of their clothing items directly from their devices.
- Support for multiple file formats (JPEG, PNG, etc.).

Categorization:
- Users can categorize their items based on type (e.g., tops, bottoms, dresses, shoes, accessories).
- Option to add tags for better organization (e.g., casual, formal, seasonal).

Detailed Descriptions:
- Users can enter descriptions for each clothing item, including brand, size, color, and fabric type.
- Option to include care instructions or notes about the item.

Editing and Updating:
- Ability to edit details of the uploaded items later (e.g., change the photo, update the description).
- Mark items as "available" or "unavailable" to manage wardrobe status.

Outfit Suggestions:
- Based on uploaded items, the system can suggest potential outfits or combinations.
- Users can save outfit combinations for future reference.

Random Outfit Generator

User's Wardrobe Integration:
- The generator pulls from a user’s uploaded or categorized clothing items, which may include tops, bottoms, dresses, shoes, and accessories.

Algorithmic Selection:
- Using algorithms or predefined rules, the generator selects a mix of items to create a complete outfit. It may consider factors like color coordination, style compatibility, and seasonal appropriateness.

Randomization:
- The core functionality of the generator is to provide random combinations, which helps users discover new ways to wear existing items. This can lead to more creative outfit choices that users might not have considered themselves.

User Preferences:
- Some generators allow users to set preferences, such as style type (casual, formal), color palettes, or specific clothing items to include or exclude from the suggestions.

Outfit Saving:
- Users may have the option to save the suggested outfits for future reference, allowing them to quickly select outfits they liked without having to generate them again.

Plan Your Week

Weekly Outfit Scheduling:
Users can select specific outfits from their wardrobe for each day of the week, allowing them to plan their looks in advance.

Calendar Integration:
The feature could integrate with a calendar application to remind users of their planned outfits for each day, making it easier to keep track of their style choices.

Flexibility and Adjustments:
Users might have the option to modify their selections throughout the week based on changes in plans, weather conditions, or personal preferences.

Themed Days:
It could allow users to create themed days (like "Casual Monday," "Formal Friday," etc.) to keep their style interesting and varied.

Suggestions Based on Events:
The tool may suggest outfits based on planned events or occasions, ensuring users are appropriately dressed for meetings, social gatherings, or other activities.
